HOW TO PROTECT
OURSELVES?

There are some
suggestions from CDC.

Choose a well-fitting Mask

✔Fit snugly against your face
✔With a nose wire
✔Mask fitter or brace is better
✔Add layers of material

Social Distance

✔Open windows and doors
✔Try to avoid crowded places

Wash hands frequently

✔With soa and water
✔At least 20 seconds
✔Use a hand sanitizer containing at least 60% alcohol
✔Don't touch your eyes, nose, and mouth before you wash your hands

Clean and disinfect

✔Clean high touch surfaces
✔Desks, phones, keyboards, handles, etc.


If you are experiencing any symptoms like fever, cough, headache, loss of taste or smell, or other COVID-liked symptoms, please take action as soon as possible.
